So let's discuss a few terms before we go into the projects below. First off, What are stages? I went over a bit in the description above but never describe what they are. Stages in simple are a guide that you should follow to bring your car, truck, or SUV to a certain point. They are usually package upgrades but sometimes a good guide to bring your car to a certain point. Some will argue that it is simply a gimmick or that it is amazing. Personally it is up to you to decide what it truly is.

Another few terns that will be common in this site will be Performance and Stance cars. A performance car is just has the name may imply, a car that is made to perform normally for racing. A stanced car is another customization style where the car is usually just for show. Most stance cars are lowered with nice rims and the tires camber "camber deals with the wheels being tilted or off center".
